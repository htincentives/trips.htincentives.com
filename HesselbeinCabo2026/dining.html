Grand Solmar Land's End Restaurants

Gourmet dining finds its home in breathtaking oceanfront settings at Grand Solmar Land's End. Our diverse selection of restaurants offers something for every palate, from traditional Mexican favorites to farm-to-table creations. Each menu showcases locally sourced ingredients and bold, exquisite flavors, served in settings that inspire. Whether it’s a casual poolside meal or an elegant al fresco dinner under the stars, every dining experience adds its own unique flavor to your Cabo adventure DON LUIS Experience refined dining with a Mexican twist at Don Luis, where fresh, local ingredients are paired with international flavors. Enjoy sweeping Pacific views as each meal becomes a culinary highlight.

LA ROCA Savor a sophisticated menu of international cuisine at La Roca, where fresh, local ingredients are transformed into culinary masterpieces. With panoramic ocean views and gourmet dishes, our flagship restaurant promises an unforgettable dining experience.

SPORTS BAR Unwind in the relaxed atmosphere of our Sports Bar, where you can enjoy a variety of international dishes while catching your favorite games on the big screen. It’s the perfect spot for casual dining or a quick bite.

ASIAN LOUNGE Indulge in the flavors of Japan at the Asian Lounge, offering fresh sushi, sashimi, and other Japanese favorites. With its serene atmosphere and stylish décor, it’s the perfect place for a unique dining experience in Cabo.

LAS BRISAS Nestled by the ocean, Las Brisas offers a laid-back atmosphere. Sip craft cocktails and savor delicious meals while taking in the stunning seaside views.

DELI CAFE Perfect for a quick bite or casual meal, the Deli Café serves light snacks, pastries, and freshly brewed coffee.
