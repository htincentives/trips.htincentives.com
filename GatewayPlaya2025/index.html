Welcome to the Mayan Riviera, boasting world-class beaches and warm turquoise waters. Playa del Carmen or Playa as it is colloquially referred to, is within a 20 minute drive via taxi. Playa del Carmen, originally a fishing village, has retained its village charm and artists colony while blending with a European influence to offer hip night spots, trendy cafes and colorful shops for the experiencing. The island of Cozumel is a short ferry ride away.

Secrets Akumal Riviera Maya is ideally located in the heart of the Riviera Maya along the famed white-sand beaches and calm clear waters of Akumal, just 20 minutes from famous Playa del Carmen Town and a little over 1 hour south of the Cancun International Airport. This adults only resort offers unlimited luxury experience with 434 elegant suites, 24-hour room service, daily refreshed mini-bar, limitless gourmet dining and top-shelf spirits at 7 restaurants and chic bars, pool and beach wait service, nightly entertainment and more. The world's second largest barrier reef sits just 150 meters off the resort's beach.

Enjoy the natural beauty of Akumal - meaning "Place of Turtles" in Mayan - by snorkeling off the coast, visiting nearby underwater rivers, or simply basking on the pristine beach. A whole new world awaits at Secrets Akumal Riviera Maya.
