DINING

Embrace the morning with a delightful breakfast amidst picturesque ocean vistas. Indulge in farm-to-table cuisine, thoughtfully sourced from Kuilima Farm, featuring a blend of traditional Hawaiian fare and distinctive creations. At our range of bars and restaurants in O'ahu, Hawaii, relish in the diverse flavors of the North Shore, fostering an atmosphere for guests to convene and partake in the authentic spirit of aloha.

Alaia
Our signature restaurant located on the lobby level highlights the island's abundant bounty with locally inspired creations. At our Beachfront O'ahu restaurants, enjoy a variety of modern dishes prepared with the freshest North Shore ingredients.

Sunset
Pull up a chair where the sun meets the surf at Sunset Pool Bar, located at the Main Resort Pool deck. Watch surfers flock to catch a wave as the sun sets below the horizon and enjoy live music entertainment on select evenings.

Ho‘olana
Aloha Kakahiaka! Start your day at our lobby coffee shop with a freshly brewed cup of coffee and beautiful ocean vistas. Enjoy oven-fresh baked goods, delicious smoothies, or grab a bite to holoholo (go cruising).

Paniolo, A Hawaiian Cowboy Lūʻau
Set against the ocean backdrop at our horse Stables, celebrate Hawai‘i’s rich ranching and cowboy heritage at our weekly dinner show. Enjoy a locally inspired buffet, cultural music and dance, and captivating tales of the Paniolo way of life.

Beach House by Roy Yamaguchi
Located at Kuilima Cove, savor welcoming, family-favorite dishes created by Hawai‘i’s famed chef Roy Yamaguchi, inspired by memories of beachside gatherings. Visit our North Shore, O‘ahu, restaurants for incredible menus and beachfront views.

Lei Lei's Bar & Grill
A local favorite, Lei Lei's Bar & Grill is celebrated for its hearty dishes, relaxed atmosphere on the Fazio Golf Course, and lively conversations with beloved host, Uncle Ian.

Off The Lip
Talk story with old and new friends at our relaxing O'ahu, Hawai'i bar located in our stunning oceanfront lobby. Delight in savory bites and deliciously crafted cocktails. Witness the North Shore's magical sunsets while you sip and stay awhile.
