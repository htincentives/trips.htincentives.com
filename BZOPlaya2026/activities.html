Luxury Sailing & Snorkel

Climb aboard a stunning Riviera Maya catamaran for luxury sailing and snorkeling in the incredible waters of the Mexican Caribbean. Discover why sailing and snorkeling in the Riviera Maya are the most popular activities for travelers from around the world. This family-friendly excursion is a perfect blend of snorkeling and paddle boarding in crystal blue waters and sailing around breathtaking reefs and coastline views from a luxurious, French-made catamaran. This is an all-inclusive Riviera Maya catamaran cruise, eat, drink and set sail for pleasure!

  • Board a luxurious catamaran for a ride around the crystal-clear waters of the Caribbean
  • Snorkel in the world’s second largest barrier reef
  • Paddleboard and steer yourself on the calm waters
  • Enjoy lunch and an open bar on your sailboat
  • Activity duration: 4 hours (not including transportation time to and from activity)



    • Xplor

    Dare to go on an adrenaline-packed adventure that takes you both underground and way above Mexico’s Caribbean jungle at Xplor Park! This attraction offers a series of fun-filled nature activities, including zip lines, amphibious vehicles, underground rafts, stalactite rivers, and hiking through caverns and grottos. Your day at this Riviera Maya landmark will get your heart pumping in no time!

  • Explore the 145-acre Riviera Maya adventure park known as Xplor
  • Zip line 147 feet above the ground, cross suspended bridges, find a secret waterslide and enjoy a refreshing water landing
  • Race in an amphibious vehicle through 3 miles of jungle trail
  • Swim and raft through subterranean caverns covered with stalactites and stalagmites
  • Recover your energy in strategic snack islands
  • Enjoy a hearty and healthy all-inclusive buffet lunch with unlimited drinks



    • Xel-ha

    Relax to the max at Xel-Ha, an eco-park that’s about enjoying everything the natural world has to offer — from the sea to the jungle. This Riviera Maya park is for people who like their adventure with a chilled out twist, as it lets you explore treetops and underwater caves alike. It’s up to you to figure out where to begin.

  • Enjoy more than 20 activities as you explore the world’s largest natural water park
  • Snorkel among schools of colorful fish
  • Get soaked on activities like river floats and cave swimming
  • Swing above the jungle canopy on rope bridges and zip lines
  • Enjoy included breakfast, lunch and all-day snacks at a choice of restaurants and bars
