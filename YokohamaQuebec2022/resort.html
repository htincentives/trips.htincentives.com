Quebec City's Chateau Frontenac has been described on websites and in travel brochures as the most photographed hotel in the world. Why is the Chateau Frontenac famous? This magnificent hotel opened its doors in 1893. The directors of the railway society wanted to encourage luxury tourism, and hoped this would induce wealthy tourists aboard their trains. Furthermore, the Chateau Frontenac was just one of a long series of chateau-style hotels to be built by the railway company.
