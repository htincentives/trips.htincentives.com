LE CHAMPLAIN RESTAURANT
Le Champlain Restaurant is a classic landmark that offers top quality cuisine thanks to the talents of Chef Stephane Modat. The eatery serves dinner and Sunday brunch. The dress code at the eatery is smart casual.


PLACE DUFFERIN
Place Dufferin Elegant buffets and sumptuous a la carte breakfast menus, overlooking the St. Lawrence River. Afternoon tea showcasing the best seasonal and regional products


LE SAM BISTRO
Le Sam Bistro showcases some of Quebec's hottest culinary trends and ingredients. The bistro menu has something for everyone: burgers, pasta, vegetarian dishes, catch of the day and decadent desserts. Stephane Modat, the restaurant chef, focuses on local products to create the menus. Be sure to taste the salmon tartare with maple taffy!

The team of talented mixologists won multiple awards and cocktail competitions. Ordering a cocktail at the bar and watching the bartenders is an entertainment in itself!


1608 WINE & CHEESE BAR
1608 Wine & Cheese Bar Inspired by the year of Quebec City's founding, this century-old bar, has seen hundreds of legendary celebrities. A place where time stands still where you can enjoy every second and admire stunning views of the St. Lawrence River. Discover exceptional products, spectacular talents and surprising local flavours.
